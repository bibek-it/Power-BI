Introduction

Welcome to your Business Intelligence Dashboard. This guide will help you understand and effectively use all the features available to analyze your data and make informed decisions.

The dashboard provides real-time insights into your business metrics, allowing you to monitor performance, identify trends, and track KPIs across your organization.

Getting Started

Logging In

  1. Navigate to the dashboard URL provided by your administrator
  2. Enter your username and password
  3. Click "Sign In"
  4. You will be directed to your personalized dashboard
Tip: Save the dashboard URL to your bookmarks for quick access.

Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard contains several key components:

Main Metrics

Large numerical displays showing your most important KPIs at a glance.

Charts & Visualizations

Interactive graphs, bar charts, line charts, and other visual representations of your data.

Data Tables

Detailed data views that can be sorted, filtered, and exported for further analysis.

Key Features

Real-Time Data Updates

Your dashboard updates automatically to reflect the latest data from your systems.

Interactive Charts

Click on chart elements to drill down into specific data points and explore details.

Custom Reports

Generate and download custom reports tailored to your specific needs.

Data Export

Export data in multiple formats including CSV, Excel, and PDF.

Scheduled Alerts

Receive notifications when metrics exceed thresholds you've defined.

Navigation Guide

Top Menu Bar

The top navigation contains links to main dashboard sections:

  • Home: Returns to your main dashboard
  • Reports: Access pre-built and custom reports
  • Analytics: Advanced analysis tools
  • Settings: Manage your preferences and account

Left Sidebar

Quick access to frequently used sections and bookmarked reports.

Breadcrumb Navigation

Shows your current location within the dashboard. Click any breadcrumb to go back to a previous section.

Using Filters

Filters help you focus on specific data subsets. Most dashboard elements support filtering.

Applying Filters

  1. Click the Filter button on any dashboard element
  2. Select your filter criteria
  3. Click "Apply" to update the visualization

Common Filters

Filter Type Use Case
Date Range Select specific time periods for analysis
Department View data for specific departments
Region Focus on particular geographic areas
Product Category Analyze specific product lines
Note: Some filters may take a few seconds to apply when dealing with large datasets.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+F) to search for specific elements on the dashboard
  • Hover over chart bars or data points for additional details
  • Combine multiple filters for more targeted analysis
  • Set up scheduled email reports to receive updates automatically
  • Use the comparison feature to analyze trends year-over-year
  • Bookmark frequently viewed reports for quick access

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is my data not updating?

A: Try refreshing your browser (F5). If the issue persists, check with your administrator to ensure data sources are connected properly.

Q: Can I share my dashboard with colleagues?

A: Yes, use the Share button to generate a link or send reports directly via email. Check your access permissions with your administrator.

Q: How far back does the historical data go?

A: Historical data retention depends on your organization's settings. Contact your administrator for specific details.

Q: Can I customize the dashboard layout?

A: Yes, click the Edit button to rearrange widgets and customize your view. Changes are saved automatically.

Q: What should I do if I encounter an error?

A: Take note of the error message and contact your support team. Provide details about what you were doing when the error occurred.
